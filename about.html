About Us

A studio built by makers, for makers.

PixelForge started in a shared apartment with two laptops and one shared conviction: most app studios ship mediocre work because they stop caring after the contract is signed. We don't.

Our Story

Nine years, 120+ apps, one obsession: craft.

We were freelancers who kept getting hired to fix apps that looked fine in a deck but fell apart in someone's hand. So we built PixelForge around a simple idea — design and engineering shouldn't be separate hand-offs, they should happen in the same room, at the same time.

Today we're a distributed team of designers, iOS/Android engineers, and product strategists who've shipped everything from seed-stage MVPs to apps used by millions.

What We Believe

The values behind every project.

Craft over speed

We'd rather ship two weeks later with something we're proud of than hit a date with something forgettable.

Design and code, together

Our designers and engineers sit in the same sprint, not sequential hand-offs. Fewer surprises, better products.

Own the outcome

We measure success in downloads, retention, and reviews — not just whether the ticket got closed.
